Andreasens Nursery Research Visit
Kemps Creek and Mangrove Mtn

Hawkesbury Institute for the Environment Research Team

May 23-27 2016

Research Visit Summary

With the help of several staff at Andreasens Nursery we were able to measure 1116 trees. This included 20 tree species/varieties across 10 different container sizes. Container volumes covered the entrire spectrum of the AS2303 Size Index range. We assessed 53 total batches from the Kemps Creek and Mangrove Mountain sites combined. Due to the large amount of available stock we were genreally able to measure 20 trees per batch for larger sizes and 45 trees per batch for smaller sizes. The research visit was completed in a total of 4 days. Using a staging area, the above and belowground visual assessments were quickly completed for all tree stock in containers up to 45L. Larger containers were then assessed by visiting individual tree stock batches within the nursery. One tree was initially assessed for each batch. If this tree failed (i.e.J-rooting) then the option was given to the production manager to assess additional trees. Measurements of size index variables and categorical shape parameters were completed for all batches that passed the above and belowground testing process.
